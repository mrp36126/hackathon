## Slide 4: The Idea

# Absa Agent
## An Autonomous Decisioning And Orchestration Layer

Absa Agent continuously interprets customer context and coordinates action across the bank.

It does four things:

1. **Detects** meaningful customer signals
2. **Decides** the next best action using rules, models, and policy guardrails
3. **Acts** through existing APIs and channels
4. **Learns** from customer, employee, and commercial outcomes

**Core principle:**  
The agent does not wait for a customer to ask. It identifies intent and orchestrates the right response.

## Slide 6: The Core Flow

# Detect. Decide. Act. Learn.

## Detect
Build a live customer context using transaction, behavioural, CRM, product, and digital engagement signals.

## Decide
Select the next best action based on intent, eligibility, consent, risk, policy, channel preference, and commercial value.

## Act
Execute through APIs into mobile app, CRM, call centre, branch, messaging, appointment booking, document upload, and product journeys.

## Learn
Track outcomes and improve future decisions based on engagement, conversion, satisfaction, complaints, opt-outs, and employee feedback.

## Slide 16: Responsible AI

# Trusted, Explainable, Consent-Aware

Absa Agent includes responsible AI controls from the start:

- Consent and preference checks before engagement
- Reason codes for customer and employee explanations
- Human escalation for complex or sensitive decisions
- Bias monitoring across income bands, regions, age groups, and customer segments
- Audit logs for signals, decisions, actions, and outcomes
- Policy guardrails for credit, affordability, vulnerability, and marketing treatment

**Principle:**  
The AI agent may recommend and orchestrate, but regulated approval decisions remain governed by Absa policy.
