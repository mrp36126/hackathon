Convergence2 / 12
Problem Statement

Customers show intent before they ask for help.

  • A tenant is preparing to buy a home.
  • A customer is showing early signs of financial strain.
  • An SME is heading into a cash flow gap.
  • A customer is likely to abandon an application.

Why Current Approaches Fail

  • Campaigns are often generic and batch-based.
  • Digital and human channels act separately.
  • Employees do not always see predictive context.
  • Insights do not reliably become executed action.

Architecture

Absa Agent orchestrates platforms. It does not replace them.

Data Layer

Customer profile, transactions, product holdings, CRM history, digital behaviour, consent, affordability, outcome data.

AI Agent / Decision Layer

Context builder, intent detector, rules engine, next-best-action ranker, guardrails, explanation generator, learning module.

API / Execution Layer

API gateway, prequalification, product eligibility, CRM task, notification, appointment, document, and analytics APIs.

Channels

Absa app, online banking, CRM, call centre, branch, email, SMS, and WhatsApp where permitted.
