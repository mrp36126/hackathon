Technical architecture: data, agent, APIs, channels, feedback
Playing
Here is the architecture in plain English: four layers, one coordinated flow. First, the data layer brings customer signals together as live context. Then the AI agent decides the next best action using rules, guardrails, and explainability. Next, existing APIs turn the decision into real execution. Then channels deliver the experience to customers and employees. Finally, outcomes feed back into the data layer so the next decision gets smarter.
